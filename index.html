About Me

Hey, I'm Arbie! It all started with playing music with highschool friends in September 2007. In 2012, I started wondering how I could be like my favorite artists - how could I package and showcase my creation to the world? My journey in audio production/mixing and music video production began and quickly became my passion.

I then studied Multimedia Production in college, and in 2019 I turned my passion from night experimentations into a day job. Now, I produce (engineer, edit, mix, and master) rock and hip-hop music, and I do freelance audio and video editing for voice-over artists and various online businesses.

When I'm not working with audio and video, I play chess (1700-1800 ELO) and basketball.
